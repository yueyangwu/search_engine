Menus

Specialties at the Crescent City Brewhouse feature innovative New Orleans cuisine - our own unique combination of traditional Louisiana ingredients and recipes with a modern flair.

Savor the finest selection of New Orleans nouvelle cuisine in a bistro atmosphere. Selections include the freshest seafood catch, including freshly shucked oysters at our famous raw oyster bar and a medley of seafood in season.

Our culinary leader is a graduate of the Culinary Institute of America , and a native of New Orleans who was raised with the love of good food that is pervasive in our city. His avant-garde creations are ready to stimulate your taste buds and assure that you have a memorable dining experience.

On your next visit to New Orleans, enjoy the sights and sounds of the French Quarter at the Crescent City Brewhouse!

Courtyard and balcony dining available

Pilsner

A light crisp and traditionally hoppy beer with a soft palate and floral bouquet. Light both in color and taste,it is The "Classic" Old World Beer that will be most familiar to the individual who prefers domestic beers.

Red Stallion

A malty, aromatic and hoppy mixture. Copper colored, this beer is medium bodied and full of flavor. Vienna Style.

Black Forest

A full-bodied dark mahogany beer, with a rich malty texture. Sparsley hopped, in the traditional Munich style.

Weiss Beer

Golden unfiltered “wheat” beer. Smooth quality with hints of banana and clove and a spicy finish.
