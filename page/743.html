      Pretzel Reese’s S’more

      May 31, 2014

      Oh gosh, I sat down to write this post and suddenly, “Oops I did it again.” popped into my head and now I don’t know if I’m feeling like a teenager again or if I kind of want to go bang my head against the wall to get that dang song out of my head. Either way, after making these peanut butter preztel s’mores I just had to make another and while I get the song out of my head I’ll just eat another pretzel reese’s s’more. Lol, no really, I am shoveling it into my face as I type. Which is messy, but as long as Cade, Mr “Don’t eat around the computers” doesn’t know…

      Anyway, back to the important stuff. We are getting ready for our first big camping trip. Ours, not mine. I grew up camping ALL the time, and I totally have missed it. But, Cade well he’d rather be in a cabin, a nice one. That’s just not how we do it at my house. My mom and dad are amazing campers, but to tell you the truth it’s all my mom. Sorry Dad, you know it’s true. That woman is the bomb.com and I don’t even care if you think that’s old school. We roughed it when we camped, tents, cooked everything on a fire, in a dutch oven or reflector oven and wait for it, often didn’t have flushing toilets. In fact, once mom even lashed a log between two trees and dug a hole. I kid you not, that was our bathroom for a week.

      And you know what? It was awesome. We rode up into the mountains on horseback, almost died a couple times as the cliffs were crazy steep and terrifying, horses dropped us off and we were 100% on our own for a week. I’ve never felt so peaceful. Yes, we couldn’t wait to take a shower or sleep in a bed, but you guys, nature is where it’s at. The air was clean, river gorgeous and clear, and it was beautiful. Now unfortunately we have zero camping gear and we want good stuff so that we can bring back the tradition, so let’s hear it, what must we buy?

      Pretzel Reese’s S’more

      Prep Time: 1 minute

      Cook Time: 2 minutes

      Yield: as many as desired

      Serving Size: 2 s'mores

      Salty, sweet, and gooey pretzel reese's s'mores

      Ingredients

      • Original Pretzel Crisps
      • Reese's Peanut Butter Cups
      • Marshmallows

      Instructions

      1. Toast your marshmallows to the desired gooey deliciousness, place a Reese's on each pretzel, top with marshmallow and another pretzel. Enjoy!
