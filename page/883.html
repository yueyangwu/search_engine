Hoverboards removed from Amazon

There have been numerous media reports of hoverboards catching on fire. For the time being, we are not recommending any hoverboards until they are proven to be safe.

Furthermore, Amazon has removed all hoverboards from their site.

As of 12 December 2015 (5pm pacific time), all five models that we reviewed are not available on Amazon’s website.

We will update this page as more information appears.
