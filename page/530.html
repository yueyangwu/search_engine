      • Ruby Skye
        $$$ Dance Clubs
        420 Mason St
        San Francisco, CA 94102
        5/13/2013
        1 check-in

        I was very excited to see Cosmic Gate! Last time I saw them at the Hollywood Palladium they were amazing! Coming from LA's club scene I was super excited to see what San Francisco's club was all about. Sadly it Ruby Skye was a huge disappointment. When my friends and I are first entered the club security was really chill, checked my ID and ticket and that was about it. Walked into the dance floor next which was small which is no problem. The one thing i did enjoy was looking around the club the decor,light features (lasers) and sound system were all on point even though the sound was turned down. As the night went on the club got more packed and then my Ruby Skye experience went down hill from there. The other club goers there were very rude and beyond wasted drunk or I'm assuming on a huge amount of E because they were non functional kept bumping into me and pushing me. Now being part of the rave and club scene in LA I was used to this but this pushing and bumping into was on a different level of rude. Next the opening DJ was a complete joke. If he is the resident DJ at Ruby Skye he should be let go of because the lack of mixing skills and knowledge of how to use a CDJ was just not there. This guy stopped a whole track just to change into a different track. Also this guy lacked energy no waves to the crowd no fist pumping or arm raising to get the crowd going or enthusiasm. Luckily his set ended at Cosmic Gate came on and saved the night a little. More non friendly people during there set forced us upstairs were there was more to dance and the chill. We ended leaving early that's how bad it was. I will not be going back to Ruby Skye, bad vibes and lack of a TranceFamily mentality no good.
