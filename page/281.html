      • Pho Ha Noi
        $ Vietnamese, Chinese, Breakfast & Brunch
        6904 Miramar Rd
        San Diego, CA 92121
        3/12/2015
        1 check-in

        Top notch customer service.****
        Friendly and Consistent

        Minus stars for parking...
        Find parking in the BACK!

        Had some work in the area so thanks to Yelp, found this mom&pop shop today! Not too busy, so understandably they're not waiting by the door ready to plop down your bowl of pho. (Personally find those places scary. More chance of having someone's thumb rinsed in your soup. Haha)

        We ordered:
        -Pipping hot egg rolls. As good as my auntie's.
        -iced coffee no condensed milk (I like it strong!
        -spicy beef salad. I know it says spicy in the name, but really it wasn't spicy. I enjoyed it anyway. Plenty of beef and extra sauce on the side.
        -beef stew with noodles. Full of flavor!
        -pho #12, I hear it was good too. No thumb rinsing. (BF's mom makes her own at home. So I'm sure she's a decent judge.)

        If you have the time, give them a chance. This might be your new Pho-fix joint! :)

        1392 E Palomar St

        3/9/2015
        5 check-ins

        Get my facial waxes & nails done here.

        Can't speak for any acrylic work. But the basic spa mani & pedi's are good and last at least a week. (Of course that depends on your activity level.) Price is reasonable too for the area.

        Eyebrow/facial waxing; I decided to save myself some $$ for a few months and did my own maintenance at home. I decided to go in one day... Thinking she took too much off, I freaked out as soon as I got to the car! [After the redness subsided and I showed my best-friend, I realized that she (the owner) did me a favor!! Needless to say, I gladly spend the $$ more regularly now.

        Tip: Don't try and call to make an appt they just don't take them. They've been burned too many times by no-shows that it's not worth it. Best to just walk-in and be patient. I guess you could call to see how busy they are... But then again a group of 5 could fill the place as soon as they hang up.

        Enjoy!
