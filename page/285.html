Opened in 1995 as the Original Pancake Palace, The Diner on Abercorn offers excellent food at reasonable prices; and is open 7 days a week, 24 hours a day, for your convenience and dining enjoyment.

Come in for breakfast, lunch, or dinner. The menu includes a wide selection of delicious choices, including salads and deserts, as well as unique specials which can only be found here.

The Diner on Abercorn is a locally operated family restaurant and is known throughout Savannah for its history of serving fine food. This is where the "locals" come - and not just for the great food, but also because of the clean comfortable atmosphere, the talented expert cooks, and the friendly professional staff.

Be sure to ask about our 10% discount policy for active members of the military, college students with valid ID, Emergency Services personnel, and senior citizens.


We accept most major bank cards
