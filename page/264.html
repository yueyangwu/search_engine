History - Angelo's Coney Island is a Michigan institution, land mark and household name. Anyone who has lived in the Michigan Area has visited Angelo's. Angelo's was started in 1949, and is proud to serve the Genesee County area and the surrounding counties for 67 years. People drive from all over to eat at this landmark.
