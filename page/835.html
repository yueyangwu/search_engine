Brian O’Reilly's unique interpretations of classic Belgian and German styles have given Sly Fox a national reputation in the world of craft brewing. Learn about all of our great beers and find out what’s on tap at our locations below.



Sly Fox Brewhouse & Eatery in Phoenixville, PA has been delighting fans of fine beers and quality dining since 1996. A second brewery opened in 2004 in response to overwhelming demand and moved to the current Pottstown location in 2012, tripling production. Sly Fox serves a loyal local customer base and attracts beer lovers from the entire Eastern Seaboard.
