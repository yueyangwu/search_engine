Located on the corner of Magazine and Pleasant Streets in the heart of New Orleans' famous Garden District and Magazine Street entertainment district, The Bulldog offers one of the best dog-friendly patios in town, complete with a large-screen TV and our famous beer-tap fountain. Enjoy any of 50 fine draft beers (mostly craft and local) or 100+ bottled beers along with a full selection of cocktails and our award-winning pub fare. As the original (and nationally recognized) location, a visit to New Orleans isn't complete without a visit to The Bulldog-Uptown.

Over 21 only
