Key features
-Made of tempered glass and steel, durable materials that give an open, airy feel.
-The shelf is black-brown on one side and black on the other, so you can choose the expression you like the best.
-Table with work surface and storage inside for a laptop turns any small space into a functional work space.
-Self-adhesive cable clips keep your cords in place and out of sight.
-Adjustable feet allow you to level the table on uneven floors.
Designer thoughts
"I believe that the objects people like - and are proud of - should really be seen in their home. So when I designed VITTSJÖ I wanted to take that idea a step further. I chose materials that really bring out books, vases and other favorite things. The straight, simple lines of the metal give the furniture a graphic expression and put an attractive frame around all your personal things. And the glass creates a light, airy feeling. I'm very happy with the results - VITTSJÖ looks great no matter what style you have in your room. And it comes at a great price, too!"
Designer Johan Kroon
