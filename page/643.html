      • Lakeside Restaurant & Bar
        $$$ American (New), Steakhouses
        600 La Torretta Blvd
        Montgomery, TX 77356
        10/27/2015

        I can only review on the happy hour experience.

        My gal pals and I were rained in so we didn't want to go anywhere away from the resort. The shuttle at La Torretta drove us over to Lakeside for happy hour. $12-13 for cocktails are not happy hour prices! $5 house wines are okay. Their house wines are from the Canyon lineup, which was pretty good.

        For the food, we ordered the crab cake... which was only 1 crab cake. Good, but not spectacular. I did like the diablo shrimp. I could've ordered another one. The seared tuna appetizer is off the regular menu and I could order it again as well.

        This place is more like a 3.5 but I bumped it up to 4 because the bartender who served us gave great service.

        .... why is there only 1 restroom stall?

      • Pho Dien 2
        $ Vietnamese
        11830 Bellaire Blvd
        Houston, TX 77072
        10/20/2015
        1 check-in

        I've never been a PHO nazi before and didn't really believe it when my hubs said this place has the best pho, but I stand corrected.

        The tai (rare beef) is nicely tenderized. Their sate is homemade and more chili rather than straight oil. The broth is high quality and flavorful.

        The only thing that sucks is that they don't serve anything else... no appetizers... no rice places..nothing.

        This is the second location and the first one is better, but sometimes you just don't want to wait over 30 minutes for a bowl of pho....

      • Lotus Seafood Restaurant
        $$ Cajun/Creole, Seafood
        2825 S Kirkwood Rd
        Houston, TX 77082
        10/20/2015
        1 check-in

        Waiting over 30 minutes for food is just not acceptable. They didn't even apologize while we were waiting for the food until the very end. It would be nice to get an update every so often to let us know why the food is taking forever. My husband noticed lots of people picking up togo orders. Perhaps they know service is super slow so they do take-out. But dine-in customers should always be prioritized over call-ins. There were several "cashiers" or "front workers" that were just standing around.

        Was the food worth the wait? Not even. The shrimp fried rice was covered in soy sauce and with probably 10 SMALL pieces of shrimp. I mean I never heard an 11-year-old complain about not enough shrimp before. The fried catfish was thin. My son LOVES fried catfish and usually kills it all at several other restaurants. He did not care for this one. The fries are not crispy (how I prefer them). My hubs ordered the lobster Louisiana fried rice that was about $24... SO NOT worth it. I rather go to Jasmine or Vinh Hoa to get the 2 lobster special for $20. This so-called Louisiana fried rice was soggy and felt like I was eating risotto.

        Their only saving grace is their so-called "crack" sauce. It is crazy addicting and good - some type of creamy butter and who knows what else. It definitely made the lobster, fries, and catfish taste better... But they are stingy with it. You only get 1 tiny side of it to go with the lobster fried rice. If you want more, expect to pay!

        They only serve bottled drinks. So if you plan on eating fatty and spicy foods, expect to pay lots of money on buying several bottles.

      • Pumpd Nutrition
        $ Weight Loss Centers, Health Markets, Juice Bars & Smoothies
        1531 Eldridge Pkwy
        Houston, TX 77077
        10/2/2015
        1 check-in

        Came here 'cause Groupon was having a special and I like to venture out to new places to find good smoothies or juices.

        The guy working was very hospitable and even though my kids only wanted plain strawberry, I have to admit it was one of the best tasting strawberry smoothies I've had. It's not creamy or gritty like how Smoothie King can be. It was almost like a mix between a slush and a smoothie.

        He was kind enough to offer to split the smoothies into two cups for my two younger kids.

        Definitely will come back to try their actual drink combos.
