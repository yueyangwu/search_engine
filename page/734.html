About Mudville

Mudville 9 has been family owned and operated since 1977. In 1991, the current concept was born. The menu back then was comprised of 10 flavors of chicken wings, hamburgers and chicken sandwiches. We have since expanded the menu to include a large assortment of American bar and grill foods including ribs, wraps, salads, starters and more.
