We at Broadway Diner would like to wish everyone a
safe and a Happy Holiday Season!

On April 25th, 2009 the Food Network's "Diners, Drive-Ins, & Dives", was on location at the Broadway Diner. The shows host, Guy Fieri, met with the owners and taped a segment titled Diners You Sent Me To. During his visit Guy found four favorite dishes on the menu and even listed the recipe for Broadway Festival on his site.

Guy recommends you try the following dishes during your next visit to the Broadway Diner:
  • Hungarian Goulash - Guy thought this dish was "Goulishious"
  • Potato Crusted Salmon
  • Shrimp Creole
