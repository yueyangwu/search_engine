Welcome to Penn Cove Shellfish!

Since 1975, it has been our mission to be the premier provider of the finest sustainably farmed shellfish products in the market today. Always fresh from the waters of Penn Cove, we grow, wet store, and distribute Penn Cove Mussels, Mediterranean Mussels, Manila clams and 27 varieties of Pacific Oysters. We only harvest AFTER your order is placed, ensuring you the absolute freshest Mussels, Oysters and Clams available Anywhere!

Throughout the website we want to give you insight about Penn Cove Shellfish and provide you with information about our products. So click on the links on the top or right side of this page to learn specific information about us and our products. Thank you for visiting Penn Cove!

We hope you enjoy visiting our website. If you have any questions or comments please call us at 888-743-2033. We would love to talk to you!
