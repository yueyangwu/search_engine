      • Gaetana’s
        $$ Italian
        143 Christopher St
        New York, NY 10014
        2/18/2014
        1 check-in

        My friend and I came here for a late lunch / early dinner at 3pm.  We ordered the baked clams for appetizers.  It came with seven tiny baby clams (the size of tea spoons).  I ordered a chicken entree and my friend had the pasta.   The pasta was in decent sized portion.  When my chicken entree came, we couldn't believe the abysmally tiny portion of the dish; it was exactly three thinly sliced bite sized chicken on a regular sized plate with one scoop of yellow rice with peas.  I would think even an appetizer portion would be bigger.  
        With that said, I would give this place a negative four stars and not recommend this place to anyone.
